Electrical Outlet Installation

Aardvark Electric has skilled technicians to install outlets and switches for your home. Our team can replace or install any of the following:

  • Outlets and switch plates
  • Dimmer switches
  • Light timers
  • Wireless switches
  • Motion-Activated light controls
  • Fan controls

    • Old, loose, or malfunctioning outlets and switches are dangerous. Be aware of the following signs:

  • Plugs that sag or fall out of the outlet
  • Buzzing sounds
  • Flickering lights
  • Outlets that spark or feel warm
